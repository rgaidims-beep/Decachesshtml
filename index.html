Decachess Rules

Board

10×10 board, files a–j, ranks 1–10.

Pieces & Movement

  • King (K) — 1 square any direction.
  • Queen (Q) — Any number of squares any direction.
  • Rook (R) — Horizontal & vertical, any number.
  • Knight (N) — Standard L-shape jump.
  • Pawn (P) — 1 square forward; first move up to 3 squares (once per pawn).
  • Wizard (W) — Bishop moves OR Knight moves.
  • Dragon (D) — Rook-style, up to 4 squares. No jumping.
  • Champion (C) — 1–2 squares any direction (no jumping).
  • Archer (A) — Moves 1 square to empty squares only. Attacks without moving, straight 1–2 squares.
  • Portal (O) — Moves 1 square; or swaps with any friendly piece (not King) within 3 squares.

Pawn Promotion

Promote to Queen, Wizard, Dragon, Champion, or Archer.

Castling

King castles with Rook or Dragon (standard rules apply).
